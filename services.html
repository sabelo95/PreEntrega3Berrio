3D MODELING SERVICES

We provide a comprehensive BIM Services with the outputs that enable clients to optimize project performance, minimize risk and deliver quality end products. Throughout the construction lifecycle, from preparation stages through to As Built.

POINT CLOUD CONVERSION

At M&S, We utilise the latest technology, We create our own revit tools to support us to convert pointclouds and laser survey data to information rich BIM models with complete details for architectural elements like doors windows, ceiling, floors and walls, for structural elements like beams, roofs, trusses, columns and many more along with MEP elements including ducts, pipes, heating, cooling and various other mechanical equipments with time-saving and cost-cutting for our clients.

3D SCANNING

When your existing project requires making changes or upgrades, you need to know that the new design, equipments will be suitable with what is already in place. Our 3D Laser Scanning services will give you the full picture, enabling accurate designs made to fit perfectly into the existing environment. We can scan inaccessible areas and make sure the whole project is captured completely and safely.

2D REVIT DRAFTING

REVIT team with many years of experience converting blue prints, architectural drawings, mechanical drawings, topographical maps and other similar images on papers into digitized format.
